Providing Thought Leadership in Organisational Innovation and Business Transformation

Established in 1981, the Institute of Systems Science at National University of Singapore (NUS-ISS) provides graduate education, executive education programmes, consultancy and research services. To date, more than 148,000 digital leaders and professionals, 7,700 corporate customers and 6,620 post-graduate alumni have benefitted from NUS-ISS’ suite of services.
